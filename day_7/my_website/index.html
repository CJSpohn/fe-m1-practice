My climbing life

I have been climbing for about 7 years. I worked as a commercial routesetter in one of the most well known bouldering gyms in the world for 2 years. I love any chance to get on the wall, rock or plywood, day or night.

Experience

Front Desk

The Spot Bouldering Gym (Boulder)

The first year of my climbing gym career was spent behind the desk greeting customers and representing the business as the one of the many faces of The Spot. I began to learn the ins and outs of the industry and decided that it was a place I desperately wanted to be.

Routesetter

The Spot Bouldering Gym (Boulder)

After my year at the desk I found my way into the routesetting department and began setting regularly as a full-time setter. This included weekly resets as well as annual competitions and events.

Assisstant Head Routesetter

The Spot Bouldering Gym (Boulder, Denver)

After a year or so we opened a second location and with that came a promotion to assisstant head routesetter where I was responsible for overseeing and performing the maintenance for both gyms as well as assissting in the day to day when my boss was out of town.
